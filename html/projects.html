Noumu

With Noumu, you can always have the right atmosphere in your home. Noumu allows you to always have the perfect temperature by controlling your homes heater systems allowing for consistently comfortable temperatures whether it's winter summer or spring. Noumus intelligent algorithms allow you to cut down on electricity bills. collect data about the weather around you and adjust your home temperature making sure you're never too hot or cold.
